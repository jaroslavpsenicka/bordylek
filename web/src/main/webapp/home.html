Time Bank

A time-based currency is an alternative currency where the unit of account/value is the person-hour or some other time unit. Contributions are equal: one hour equals one hour. No exchange rates. Simple.

Community

Enough virtual friends and imaginary companions. This is real, as real as people from your district can be. Help someone with his garden. Accept someone else's lift from the pub. All free of charge. Only time counts.

Not a party type?

Community does not necessarily mean to have your house full of drunk people every Friday. Just stay in touch with your neighborhoods and be ready to give and accept help. That's more than enough.

Start by signing up with some of your accounts. The server does not collect any personal data including passwords and does not distribute any form of spam. We do not contact your friends.
